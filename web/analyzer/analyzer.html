DNA Analyzer Help

Getting Started

To analyze DNA sequences, follow these steps:

  1. Upload a DNA sequence file (.fa, .fasta, .fastq, .txt) by dragging it to the upload area or clicking to browse.
  2. Select the analysis options you want to run on your sequence.
  3. Click "Start Analysis" to process your sequence.
  4. View the results and download them if needed.

Analysis Options

DNAnalyzer offers several types of analysis:

  • Basic Analysis: Calculates sequence length, GC content percentage, and nucleotide composition.
  • Codon Analysis: Identifies start codons (ATG), stop codons (TAA, TAG, TGA), and reading frames.
  • Advanced Features: Performs coverage analysis, protein prediction, and promoter sequence detection.
